About Me:

Hey! Thanks for stopping by. I'm a professional chef of 10 years dipping my feet into the tech world with the goal of showing my community the power of a more decisive, goal-oriented mindset in tech.

I'm passionate about dismantling competitive systems in our society and replacing them with sustainable cooperative ones. I live in West Philadelphia at Philadelphia Service Cooperative with (currently) 7 other awesome individuals and spend my time cooking for my friends, advocating gaming in my community, and, now, learning how to code!
